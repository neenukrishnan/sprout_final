Sustainable paper cups

Reassuring our customers with our environmental commitment all of Sprout paper cups are sourced from sustainably managed forestry and their paper board is all PEFC certified. Our cups are water coated with water base emulsion certified and approved by the Government of Kerala. Our cups are 100% biodegradable, recyclable and compostable. Not only do we focus on our responsibility for our raw materials, but we have consciously decided to use only water based inks during the printing of our branded paper cups, as well as our stock design, this alternative to acrylic based inks means that our printing process is much more environmentally considerate.
